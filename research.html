Research

I research ways humans and AI can interact effectively with each other. Specifically, I aim to create interfaces that can help anyone understand and criticize faulty AI systems and improve them. Here are my 2 main avenues of research interest :


(1) Explainble AI : Improving the communication of machine learning systems in order to make them more publicly understandable (Explainable AI, XAI)


(2) Implicit Interaction : Helping AI systems understand the nuances of Human Implicit Interaction
