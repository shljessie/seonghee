May 2021 - August 2021

Data Analyst & ML Engineer of Conversational AI System



Eternal Testimony is a Conversational AI Exhibition recording the live testimonies of the former Comfort Women, victims of the Japanese War.

As of Jun 2020, there are only 17 survivors of the Japanese military comfort women. We are initiating the '영원한 증언 Eternal Testimony' project, a novel way to preserve our history for all perpetuity in a vivid and memorable way. Inspired by NDT project at USC (https://sfi.usc.edu/collections/holocaust/ndt) for holocaust survivors, the 'Eternal Testimony' project records the testimony of the victims of Japanese military comfort women in a new way. Inspired by NDT project at USC (https://sfi.usc.edu/collections/holocaust/ndt) for holocaust survivors, the 'Eternal Testimony' project records the testimony of the victims of Japanese military comfort women in a new way. With artificial intelligence technology, we offer a virtual experience of meeting and interacting with them in person. This project, started in 2018, is on-going project and is currently sponsored by Ministry of Gender Equality and Family. In 2020, I held a pilot beta exhibit in the LeeJuYeon Gallery at Sogang University.



I managed the speech recognition AI system for the Beta Exhibition of Eternal Testimony - an immersive hologram interview experience of Comfort Women. Improved the Speech Recognition AI system QA system, data analysis of the system reports, and creation of infographics for responses. Focused on handling arbitrary and nuanced questions with multiple responses to create a more immersive and natural experience for visitors and reduce repetition.

The official world-wide exhibit of this exhibition will be held in 2022
